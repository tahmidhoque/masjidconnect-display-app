The challenge

Keeping screens right shouldn't be a weekly chore

Most masajid run their displays on a TV slideshow, a free app, or a one-off setup a volunteer built. They all share the same problem: someone has to remember to change them — every week, before every event, the moment prayer times shift.

Today The manual treadmill

✕The Friday rush. Content has to be swapped for Jumu'ah every single week — and checked that it actually worked before the jama'ah arrives.
✕No scheduling. Nothing changes on its own. Every update is hands-on, in the moment, by whoever is around.
✕Times drift out of date. Daylight saving, Ramadan and seasonal changes mean prayer times need re-entering by hand.
✕Ongoing subscriptions. A monthly fee that never ends, with features locked behind higher tiers.
✕Single point of failure. When the internet drops or that one volunteer is away, the screen goes blank or wrong.

With MasjidConnect Set it once

✓Schedule Jumu'ah forever. Build the Friday layout once and it appears automatically every Friday — and reverts on its own afterwards.
✓Plan weeks ahead. Ramadan, fundraisers, Eid, school holidays — queue them up by date and time, then forget about them.
✓Prayer times calculate themselves. Set location and method once; DST and seasonal shifts are handled automatically.
✓One-off cost. A lifetime software licence you own — no per-screen monthly subscription.
✓Runs offline. Prayer times and content are cached on the device — fullest experience on our Display Unit; the screen keeps going when the internet drops.

Why "from" — and when the price goes up

  • More screens: Each package includes one installed screen. Most masajid want 2–4 (main hall, sisters' area, entrance). Additional installed screens are from £349 each — each adds mounting, wiring, pairing, and on-site time.

  • Your building: Standard wall mount and fused spur are included. Major electrical work, heritage buildings, or unusual cable runs are quoted after a site check.

Third-party devices — what care plans do and don't cover

If you pair an Android stick, smart TV, or your own PC, a care plan session can still help your volunteers with settings and content — but stick freezes, TV browser updates and laptop failures are yours to resolve. What counts as a help session?

One issue we work on with you, up to 30 minutes. Settings, content, pairing, prayer-time CSVs, and remote troubleshooting on paired screens use your plan's sessions. The same issue within a week still counts as one session. Software bugs are fixed under Platform Support and do not use sessions.

What if we need help but don't want a care plan?
You still have Platform Support for software faults on any paired device (email, 5 working days). Care plans add remote help with settings and content, and you can book paid sessions for one-off setup help. Many masajid self-serve using our guides; others choose Care after their first Ramadan.
Why are installed packages "from" prices?
Because every masjid is different. One screen in Oxfordshire is not the same job as six screens in Birmingham with long cable runs. We quote in writing after understanding your screen count, locations, travel, and building — before you book. Multi-screen rollouts are priced for the labour involved; we do not subsidise large installs at single-screen rates.
Can we upgrade later?
Yes. Upgrade your licence tier from the admin dashboard when you need more screens or features. Contact us for multi-site or network pricing if you manage several masajid.
What about refunds?
14-day money-back guarantee on first-time lifetime licence purchases, subject to our Refund Policy. Installed packages are scoped and scheduled separately — your written quote will state the terms that apply.
Where is our data held?
MasjidConnect is a UK-operated platform. Admin data and content are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure. Display devices cache content locally for offline operation; they do not expose inbound access to your network. A full privacy policy is available on our website.
We're migrating from another system — can you help?
Yes. We can import prayer times and help rebuild playlists during install or a care-plan session. Tell us what you use today on your discovery call and we will scope migration as part of your quote.
Who owns our content and data?
Your masjid owns the content you publish. MasjidConnect stores admin data and media on secure UK-hosted cloud infrastructure. You can export your data; we do not hold your masjid hostage. See our privacy policy at masjidconnect.co.uk/privacy for retention and GDPR details.
